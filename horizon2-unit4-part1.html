Bingo

This is a warmup activity which uses the important new vocab from Unit 1. It is best to give students the bingo sheet the lesson before so that they have time to fill it out for the start of the next lesson.

Wordsearch

This wordsearch uses some of the important new vocab from Unit 1. This activity can be used either as a warmup or to fill in time at the end of the lesson.

Concept Map

Simple concept map. Students have to either use "have to" or "don't have to" regarding the phrases in the boxes. They can then try to make sentences based on this information.

Have to / Don't have to - Guessing game

Read through the sheet and let students guess if the statements are "have to" or "don't have to". Some of the words/sentences may need to be explained in greater detail for the students to understand the meaning.
Then go through the answers.
