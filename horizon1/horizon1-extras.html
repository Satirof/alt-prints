Christmas Review (2nd term grammar)

Students work in groups of 3-4 students. Each group gets one A3 game sheet. They have to answer the questions, and then one student from each group comes to the teacher at the front of the class and tells them the answer. If they are correct they can move to the next question. If they are wrong they have to go back and try again. Each student in the group takes turns tellin the teacher the answer.
For the final question all students must come to the front and answer based on what they want.

You can make it a competition and have the students of the group to complete the worksheet first receive a prize.
