Spongebob's Day Activity

Start by having the students form lunch groups and give them each a set of cards (page 2) pre-cut. The students must arrange the cards in any order they want and then decide on what spongebob is doing.
E.g.: Spongebob woke up. Spongebob ate breakfast. Spongebob watched TV.

After that students work by themselves and write their own sentences on the first page of the worksheet. They should have a better idea of what to write after listening/participating in the group discussion.
