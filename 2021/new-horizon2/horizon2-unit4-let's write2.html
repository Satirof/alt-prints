Activity 125日06月2021年

This is a concept map style writing activity for students to write a letter to their first year junior high teacher, or their club teacher.

    Instructions:
  • Students first need to pick who they are going to write to.
  • Next they need to fill in the top concept map in Japanese using keywords.
  • Next translate the top concept map into English, again just using keywords.
  • Lastly they need to write full sentences based on what is in their concept maps.
  • This can be used to make a presentation in front of the class if needed also.
  • Note: The third page of the pdf is two different examples that students can use to get inspiration/help from.
