Activity 125日06月2021年

This is a mix and match activity for students to make original sentences.

    Instructions:
  • First students need to connect the left and write sides together in any way they like.
  • Next students use the information from part 1 to make full sentences in English, and then translate them into Japanese.

Activity 225日06月2021年

This is an interview activity for students to ask how they feel about doing certain things.

    Instructions:
  • First check the meaning of the left side. And then write their own answers.
  • Next have students interview each other while recording the results.
  • Lastly students need to write three sentences using the information fromt he interview, and then translate into Japanese.
