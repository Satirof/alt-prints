Activity 115日07月2021年

This is a self-introduction worksheet.

    Instructions:
  • First students need to write about themselves. Including their name, what they like, and what they are good at.
  • Next they can spend some time drawing a picture of themselves.
  • Lastly students need to introduce themselves, what they like, and what they are good at to other students in their class. They can write information they hear in the table at the bottom.
