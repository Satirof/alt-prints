Activity

This worksheet starts with simple translation of Japanese to English, and then moves into a simple interview exersize.
The next part is split into an A/B sheet for students to work together in pairs. Firstly they have various verbs required at the top (although not all the verbs), so it is best to go over the meaning of these so that they have all the vocab they need for the activity.
Next, they have to ask each other what various characters are doing, and then record the results in the space provided below.

E.g.:
A: "What is Goku doing?"
B: "Goku is drinking juice".
