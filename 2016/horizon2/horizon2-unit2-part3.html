Mix and Match

The first page of this PDF is for at the end of the activity.

For the main activity students need to work in groups of about 3-4 students. They all start with the cards marked '1'. They have to then make the grammatically correct sentence. Once they do they have to show and say the sentence to the teacher. If it is wrong they have to go back to their desk and try again. If they are correct they then move to the next set of cards '2'. Rinse and repeat until they get through all cards.

Keep a tally of where each group is on the board to make it a competition to see who finishes first.

Note: This activity requires a lot of preparation work in the way of cutting out all the cards and organizing them, as each group will need their own set, seperated by which number cards they are on.
