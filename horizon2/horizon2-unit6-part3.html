Concept Map

Students listen to the example story about pokemon, and then try to write sentences based on their memory and the provided keywords.
The story goes as follows

  • 1: Last night I enjoyed playing Pokemon.
  • 2: Because it was late,
  • 3: I stopped playing it.
  • 4: In bed, I finished playing Pokemon.
