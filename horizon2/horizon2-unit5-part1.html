Wordsearch

Matches phrases

Students start by drawing lines between the phrases. They then write full sentences below based on the lines they drew.

If the PDF below does not display properly, please click here to directly download.


Mix and Match

Students are formed into groups of 3-4. They are each given a set of cards (numbered for each group). They then have to try to make sentences using these cards. They can be mixed and matched into many different ways. They also have to pick some of their chosen sentences and write them on page 1 of the worksheet, with the translation underneath.

The second page of the worksheet is for the students to complete on their own from their own thoughts.
